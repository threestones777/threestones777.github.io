Thanks for using tReader ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your data.

Privacy Policy in short

We do not collect, use, nor disclose your data. Network transmission only used to download tReader to your device and checking for updates. Your text files and how you read them are stored locally without sending them to our servers. We do not have any resources to store your data. So we do not have any data can be share or sell. We do not use cookies nor any tracking service to identify you.

However, this site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub Inc. or GitHub B.V. may record any network access from your device. For more information about GitHub's security practices, see GitHub Privacy Policy.

By using third party online text-to-speech service, your text will be sent to their server. You should check their privacy statements before using these features.

Information we collect

We collect nothing. Your settings and books are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any remote server.

Third parties Privacy Policy

GitHub Pages

The web app is hosted on GitHub Pages. So any network requests to download this web app is transferred and processed by GitHub Pages. Network requests happened whenever the app is downloaded. That includes the first time you visit this site or whenever this site is updated. A regular update checking will also be applied. For more information about GitHub's security practices, see GitHub Privacy Policy.

Online text-to-speech service

By using text-to-speech feature, with any voices marked as "online" or "remote", your text would be sent to some third party servers so they can be converted into speechs. The voices used is installed in your system or browser. You should check any privacy statements before using these voices.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may change the Privacy Policy from time to time. You may get updated version of Privacy Policy on this page.

Contacting us

Questions about our Privacy Policy may be directed to our issue tracker.

Footnotes

This privacy policy only applied to pages hosted on https://tiansh.github.io/reader/. You may need to adjust this document if you fork this project or host is somewhere else. You may need ask the service provider for their privacy policy if you are visiting tReader from elsewhere.
